## Decisions Made
- **TrendSection kept with hardcoded 14-day period:** The backend `/events/trend` endpoint does not exist or does not accept date parameters. The `useMarketplaceTrend` hook calls the endpoint with no params. Rather than adding a backend change (out of scope for this frontend-only plan), TrendSection remains as-is.
- **SearchesPage and AdminMarketplacePage not deleted:** Their content was inlined into DataPage. The files remain on disk as they may serve as reference or could be removed in a cleanup pass.
- **Date-to-days conversion for marketplace:** When custom date range is set, it's converted to a `days` count for DemandSection and ExposureSection hooks which accept a `days` parameter.
- **Preset buttons match OverviewPage aesthetic:** Same pill-button style (bg-purple-600 for active, slate-400 for inactive) for visual consistency.

### 2. Payload Key Mismatch — explorer_click (Bug)

**File:** `app/routers/admin/leads.py` lines 162-163
**Issue:** Backend reads `payload_data.get("expert", "")` for `explorer_click` events, but frontend `ExpertCard.tsx` sends the key as `expert_id`. Result: explorer_click entries in admin lead timeline always show blank expert name.

**Affected requirements:** TRACK-03 (partial — explorer_search path works correctly)

**Fix:** Change `payload_data.get("expert", "")` to `payload_data.get("expert_id", "")` in `leads.py`.

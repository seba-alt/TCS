### Notable Implementation Decisions (Confirmed in Code)

- **Conversation archiving intentionally skipped:** Expert data is embedded in conversation JSON blobs; soft-deleting the Expert record is sufficient. Historical conversations remain intact. Consistent with plan's discretionary note.
- **Photo proxy unfiltered:** `GET /api/photos/{username}` has no `is_active` filter — admin can still proxy photos for inactive experts. Intentional per plan.
- **Already-inactive experts excluded from `to_delete`:** Idempotent — re-uploading same CSV won't re-delete already-deleted experts.
- **Old `import-csv` endpoint preserved:** Backward compatible; both old and new flow coexist on the router.
- **Hourly Rate handled separately from `_SYNC_FIELDS`:** Float comparison requires dedicated parsing logic; correctly split out in both sync-preview and sync-apply.
